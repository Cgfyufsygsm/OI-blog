P2627 [USACO11OPEN]Mowing the Lawn G && P2034 选择数字

\[ f(i,0)=\max\lbrace f(i-1,0),f(i-1,1)\rbrace \]

\[ f(i,1)=\max_{j\in(i-k,i)}\lbrace f(j,0)+s_i-s_j\rbrace=\max_{j\in(i-k,i)}\lbrace f(j,0)-s_j\rbrace+s_i \]
